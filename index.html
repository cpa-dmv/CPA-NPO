About the Foundation

Why we exist — and where we're going

We believe every dedicated CPA deserves the tools, mentorship, and community to build a thriving, independent firm. We close that gap.

Our Mission

To empower new and growing CPA firms with the education, mentorship, technology, and resources they need to launch confidently and scale sustainably.

Our Vision

A profession where independent CPA firms of every size flourish — supported by a generous community that shares knowledge, opportunity, and growth.

Core Values

Integrity, generosity, excellence, and community. We lead with service and measure success by the firms we help build.

Our journey

Founded
